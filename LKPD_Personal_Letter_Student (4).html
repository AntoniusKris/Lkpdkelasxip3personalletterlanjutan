📝 SAMPLE LETTER
Jl. Melati No. 12, Bandung
15 October 2024

Dear Sarah,


How are you doing? I hope you and your family are all in good health. I've been thinking about you a lot lately, especially since we haven't talked in ages!


I'm writing to share some exciting news — I got accepted into the university I've been dreaming of! I still can't believe it. It's been a rollercoaster of emotions, from panic during the entrance exams to pure joy when I saw the results. I really wish you were here to celebrate with me.


By the way, do you remember our old plan to travel to Lombok together? I think we should finally make it happen next year. Let me know what you think!


I miss you so much and I really hope we can meet up soon. Please write back when you get the chance.


Lots of love,
Dina

📝 Writing Tips

• Use informal, friendly tone
• Show emotions freely
• Simple present for now, past tense for past events
• Keep it natural and flowing
